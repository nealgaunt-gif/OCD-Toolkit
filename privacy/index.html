Privacy Policy — OCD Toolkit

Last updated: May 2026

OCD Toolkit is a personal wellness app designed to help people manage OCD using Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP) techniques and thought logging. Your privacy is important to us.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. OCD Toolkit stores all information — including thought logs and ERP actions — locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. There is no data to share.

Analytics & Tracking

OCD Toolkit does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Children's Privacy

OCD Toolkit does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.
