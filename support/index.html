OCD Toolkit — Support

OCD Toolkit is an iOS app that helps people manage OCD using Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP) techniques and thought logging.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device and never sent anywhere. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the app from your device removes all data permanently.

The app isn't working as expected

Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, email us with a description of what happened and which iPhone model and iOS version you're using.
